## Community Guidelines

<Steps>
  <Step title="Be Respectful">
    Treat all community members with respect and courtesy. Help create a welcoming environment for everyone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay On Topic">
    Keep discussions relevant to PhoneClaw and Android automation. Use appropriate channels for different topics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="No Spam">
    Avoid posting promotional content, spam, or repeated messages. Share helpful content thoughtfully.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Help Others">
    If you can answer someone's question or solve their problem, please do! We all benefit from a helpful community.
  </Step>
</Steps>
